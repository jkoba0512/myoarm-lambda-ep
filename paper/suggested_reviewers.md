# Suggested reviewers — Bio Cyb submission (DRAFT)

## Selection criteria

- **Topic fit**: λ-EP hypothesis / virtual trajectory / visuomotor feedback /
  spinal reflexes / Hill-type musculoskeletal modelling / MyoSuite-class
  simulation; ablation-style methodological work in motor control.
- **Diversity**: at least three countries, no two from the same lab.
- **Active**: with publications relevant to one of the above within the last
  ~5 years.
- **Seniority**: associate professor or above (or industry equivalent).
- **No conflict**: not a coauthor of the corresponding author within the
  last 5 years; not currently on the Bio Cyb editorial board.
